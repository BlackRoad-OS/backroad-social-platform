## The BackRoad Way

We're building something different:

### Core Principles

1. **No Vanity Metrics**
   - No like counts
   - No follower counts
   - No public view metrics
   - No "trending" or "viral" feeds

2. **Meaningful Connections**
   - Deep conversations, not hot takes
   - Knowledge sharing, not performative posting
   - Private threads, not public broadcasts
   - Quality over quantity

3. **User Respect**
   - No algorithmic manipulation
   - No dark patterns
   - No data selling
   - No attention hijacking
   - Chronological feeds always

4. **Depth Metrics That Matter**
   - **Conversation depth** - How many meaningful exchanges?
   - **Knowledge shared** - Did you teach/learn something?
   - **Connection quality** - Do you *actually* talk to your connections?
   - **Time well spent** - Did you leave enriched, not drained?
